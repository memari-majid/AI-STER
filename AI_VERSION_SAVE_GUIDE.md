## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Navigate to "📝 New Evaluation"** in the sidebar

2. **Fill in the required information:**
   - Student Teacher Name
   - Evaluator Name
   - Select evaluation type (Field or STER)
   - Evaluator role

3. **Add Observation Notes** (Step 3):
   - Type or paste detailed observation notes
   - The more detail, the better the AI analysis

4. **Generate AI Analysis:**
   - Click the blue button: **"🤖 Generate AI Analysis & Begin Scoring"**
   - Wait for the spinner to complete (takes 10-30 seconds)

5. **LOOK TO THE RIGHT** after AI completes:
   - You'll see a green success message: "✅ AI Analysis Complete"
   - **DIRECTLY BELOW THAT** is the **"💾 Save AI Version"** button
   - Click it BEFORE making any modifications!

6. **After clicking Save AI Version:**
   - You'll see: "✅ AI version saved! It will be preserved when you save the evaluation."
   - The button changes to: "📌 AI original version saved"
   - NOW you can modify scores and justifications

## 🔍 Where Data is Saved

The AI-generated content is saved in **two stages**:

1. **Temporarily** (when you click "Save AI Version"):
   - Stored in session memory
   - Will be lost if you close the browser without saving the evaluation

2. **Permanently** (when you save as draft or complete):
   - Saved to `data_storage/evaluations.json`
   - Includes the complete AI original version
   - Preserved for research comparison

## 📊 Viewing Saved AI Versions

After saving evaluations with AI versions:
1. Go to **"🔬 Research Comparison"** in the sidebar
2. Select an evaluation with saved AI version
3. View side-by-side comparisons of AI vs Supervisor modifications

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- The button ONLY appears AFTER AI analysis is complete
- Save the AI version BEFORE making ANY modifications
- You can only save the AI version once per evaluation
- If you refresh the page before saving the evaluation, the AI version will be lost

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Can't see the button?**
- Make sure you've completed the AI analysis first
- Look in the RIGHT column, not the main form area
- The button appears below "AI Analysis Complete"

**Button disappeared?**
- You may have already clicked it (look for "📌 AI original version saved")
- Or you refreshed the page (AI version data was lost)

**Need to start over?**
- Click "🔄 Regenerate Analysis" to create new AI content
- Then save the new AI version
