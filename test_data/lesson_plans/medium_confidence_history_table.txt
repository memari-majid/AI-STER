+------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Topic: The American Revolution - Causes and Key Events            |
| Duration: 45 minutes                                              |
| Class Size: 28 students                                           |
+------------------------------------------------------------------+

STANDARDS ALIGNMENT:
• Utah Social Studies Standard 7.US.1.3: Explain the major ideas and events leading to the American Revolution

OBJECTIVES:
Students will be able to:
1. Identify three major causes of the American Revolution
2. Sequence key events from 1765-1776
3. Analyze primary source documents from the period

MATERIALS & RESOURCES:
□ Textbook Chapter 5
□ Primary source packet (Boston Tea Party accounts)
□ Timeline template handouts
□ Chromebooks (class set)
□ Video clip: "Liberty's Kids" episode 1 (5 min)

LESSON SEQUENCE:
┌─────────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Time        │ Activity                                            │
├─────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ 0-5 min     │ Bell Ringer: "No taxation without..."              │
│ 5-10 min    │ Video clip introduction                             │
│ 10-20 min   │ Direct instruction: Causes of Revolution            │
│ 20-35 min   │ Group work: Primary source analysis                 │
│ 35-42 min   │ Share findings & create timeline                    │
│ 42-45 min   │ Exit ticket: Name one cause of the Revolution      │
└─────────────┴────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

ASSESSMENT STRATEGIES:
• Formative: Observation during group work, exit ticket responses
• Summative: Timeline project due Friday, Chapter test next week

ACCOMMODATIONS:
• IEP students: Modified primary sources, peer support
• Advanced: Additional challenge document on economic factors
• ELL: Vocabulary sheet with illustrations, partner with strong reader
