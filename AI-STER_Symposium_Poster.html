🎯 Problem & Solution

Educational Challenge

Traditional student teacher evaluations face significant challenges:

  • Time-Intensive: Manual evidence extraction and justification
  • Compliance: Meeting Utah State Board of Education standards
  • Documentation: Insufficient evidence-based feedback

🤖 AI-Supported Solution

STER App directly addresses our current evaluation process needs by generating draft justifications and organizing rubric items aligned to USBE standards. Supervisors remain in full control of evaluations while benefiting from AI-assisted documentation.

🏆 Current Status: Pilot testing at Utah Valley University • Faculty and supervisors currently testing • Feedback being collected from early users • IRB approval submitted for broader research

⚡ Pilot Features

🤖 Draft Justification Generation

Pilot feature: AI-assisted draft justifications from lesson plans and observation notes

📋 USBE-Aligned Rubrics

Field (8 items) and STER (35 items) evaluations organized per Utah standards

⚖️ Ethical AI Design

Human-centered approach with supervisor control over all evaluations and decisions

📊 Progress Tracking

Pilot feature: Dashboard for tracking evaluation progress and completion status

🌐 Web-Based Interface

Pilot feature: Browser-based access for easier adoption and testing

🔒 Privacy-First Approach

Designed with FERPA compliance and data security in mind

🛡️ Responsible AI Principles

  • Transparency: AI suggestions clearly marked and editable
  • Human Agency: Supervisors retain final scoring authority
  • Bias Mitigation: Evidence-based prompts reduce subjective bias
  • Privacy Protection: No student data used for AI training
  • Accessibility: Designed for all users, including those new to AI
